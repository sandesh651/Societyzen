How the product works

From bill creation to ledger update in four simple steps.

SocietyZen is designed for a clear billing-to-payment journey that residents and admins can follow easily.

01

Create bills

Generate maintenance bills unit-wise and keep bill records ready for sharing or printing.

02

Share QR or payment link

Residents receive a QR code or link that opens the payment page on mobile.

03

Collect payments

Payments can be made online while your application continues to manage society data.

04

Update ledger & reports

Post-payment status, bill records and summaries can reflect the latest collection activity.
